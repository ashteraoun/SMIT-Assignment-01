Naseeb Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb mutton, cut into pieces
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 onions, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/4 cup oil
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro and mint leaves, chopped
  • Saffron strands soaked in warm milk
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cloves, cinnamon, cardamom)

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil and fry the onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnishing.
  3. Add whole spices and ginger-garlic paste. Saut√© for a minute.
  4. Add mutton pieces, turmeric, red chili powder, cumin, coriander, and salt. Cook until the mutton is browned.
  5. Add yogurt, tomatoes, and green chilies. Cook until the oil separates.
  6. Add chopped cilantro and mint leaves. Cook for a few more minutes.
  7. In another pot, boil water and cook the rice until 70% done. Drain the water.
  8. Layer the partially cooked rice over the mutton. Drizzle saffron milk, ghee, and fried onions on top.
  9. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat (dum) for 20-25 minutes.
  10. Garnish with fresh cilantro and mint leaves before serving.
